# Messaging & Tools Fixes (Apply ALONGSIDE ROUND2_FIXES.md)

## 1. UNIFY THE MESSAGING — "Revenue Recovery Through Operations Automation"

The core message should be: "Distributors lose revenue because their operations are manual. We automate the operations, which recovers the revenue."

The narrative flow should be:
- Manual quoting = slow response = lost deals
- No customer health tracking = silent churn = lost revenue  
- Unoptimized routes = wasted fuel + missed delivery windows = unhappy customers
- No visibility = can't see problems until they become revenue losses

So the UNIFIED pitch is: "We automate your operations so you stop bleeding revenue."

### Specific messaging changes:
- Hero: Frame automation AS the path to revenue recovery (not two separate things)
- Problem cards: Each problem should connect manual work → revenue loss
- Services: Position automation services as revenue-generating, not just cost-saving. E.g., "Automated quoting doesn't just save time, it means you never lose a deal because a quote took too long"
- Case studies: Each should show automation → revenue result (not just "we automated X" but "we automated X which generated $Y")
- Trust section: Unify around "operations automation that pays for itself"

### The unified tagline/angle:
"Every manual process in your operation is revenue walking out the door."
